Hi, I'm Nikki, a student in Info 474. This is my interactive portfolio where I'll share class projects and visualizations.

Clock 1 Prototype

The runner goes around a circular track once every hour. The angle shows how far the runner is into the current hour (minute + seconds). The runner dot turns from cool to hot pink as they get tired throughout the hour. Background gets lighter in later hours of the day. This is meant to feel like a training loop.

Clock 2 Prototype

This clock treats each minute like a sprint rep. The teal bar fills left to right over the 60 seconds. Above it is digital time. This feels more like interval pacing. The runner can instantly see how deep they are into the current rep.

Clock 3 Prototype

A full loop route (like running around Yellow Lake in Issaquah). The marker moves around the loop as the minute and second pass. The checkpoints act like mile markers and light up when you pass them. The digital time sits at the bottom like a race clock.
